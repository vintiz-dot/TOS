Two Types of Changes

Physical

Changes you can see on the outside

  • Growing taller & heavier
  • Learning to walk and run
  • Hair color changing with age
  • Muscles getting stronger

Mental

Changes happening inside the brain

  • Learning how to speak
  • Solving harder problems
  • Understanding your feelings
  • Making smart decisions

⚠️ Remember: Development is NOT just getting bigger — your brain changes too!

The Early Years

👶

Infancy

0 – 1 Year

Very dependent on adults. Can breathe and cry but cannot speak yet.

🧒

Childhood

1 – 12 Years

Starts as a toddler. Constantly growing & learning: walking, talking, eating.

🧑‍🎓

Adolescence

12 – 19 Years

Teenagers! Rapid growth. Puberty causes major external & internal changes.

Growing Up & Beyond

👩‍💼

Adulthood

20 – 65 Years

  • Young adults: 20–36 years
  • Middle-aged: 36–55 years
  • Older adults: 55–65 years
  • Most independent stage
  • Have children of their own

👴

Elderly

65+ Years

  • The final stage of human growth
  • Hair may turn gray
  • Physical strength changes
  • Brain keeps learning! (wisdom)
