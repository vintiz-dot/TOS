The Math Shape Park

Moving Shapes Project

Things you need: Big Paper, Pencil, Ruler, Eraser

About the Project

The Story: You are the boss of a new fun park. The park needs to look nice. You must use math to move and change shapes to build the park.

Your Job: First, draw a big shape with 6 corners. This is the Main Square. Next, move, flip, turn, and change the size of this shape to make other park areas. You must write down all your math points.

Step 1: Draw the Map

  1. Draw the Lines: Use your ruler to draw the X line and Y line on your paper.
  2. The Main Square (Start Shape): Draw a shape with 6 corners. Name the corners A, B, C, D, E, and F. Write down their points (x, y).
  3. The Water Park (Reflection / Flip): Flip the Main Square over the X line or the Y line.
  4. The Fast Rides (Translation / Slide): Slide the Main Square (for example: go up 5, go left 8).
  5. The Big Wheel (Rotation / Turn): Turn the Main Square around the center point (0,0).
  6. The Big Gate (Dilation / Make Bigger): Make the Main Square bigger.
  7. The Food Shops (Dilation / Make Smaller): Make the Main Square smaller.

Helpful Math Rules

Slide (Translation): Move the shape.
Rule: $(x, y) \rightarrow (x+a, y+b)$
Flip (Reflection over X): Flip like a mirror. Rule: $(x, y) \rightarrow (x, -y)$
Flip (Reflection over Y): Flip like a mirror. Rule: $(x, y) \rightarrow (-x, y)$
Turn (Rotation 90° CW): Spin the shape. Rule: $(x, y) \rightarrow (y, -x)$
Turn (Rotation 180°): Spin the shape. Rule: $(x, y) \rightarrow (-x, -y)$
Change Size (Dilation): Make it bigger or smaller.
Rule: $(x, y) \rightarrow (kx, ky)$ where k is the size number.
